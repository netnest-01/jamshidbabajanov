About me

I, Jamshid Bobojonov, was born on June 15, 1987, in the city of Tashkent. In 1993, I entered the first grade of Secondary School No. 91. In 2002, I was admitted to the Faculty of International Economic Relations at Tashkent State University of Oriental Studies (Bachelor's). From 2007 to 2009, I studied at this institute (Master's). I have one child (I am divorced). From 2009 to 2015, I worked at the Central Bank of the Republic of Uzbekistan.                

From 2020, I started my acting and food vlogging career. In 2021, I hosted the "Oydin hayot live" program on the National Television and Radio Company of the Republic of Uzbekistan (O'zbekiston TV channel). In 2022, I hosted the "Issiq non Tongi" program on Renisans TV. In 2023, I hosted the "Jamlangan Taomlar" program on Zo'r TV. In 2023, I hosted the "Jambul-jam" program on Yoshlar TV. In 2024, I participated in "Baraban Sketshow" on Mening Yurtim TV. In 2025, I am hosting the "Yangi tong" program on Renisans TV.

Film and TV series

Actor

In 2020, I started my acting career. My first project was the TV series "Meni Asra" (Save Me), where I participated as Jasur under the direction of Bakhtiyor Safarov. Then I was invited to Durdona Tolaganova's films "Sensiz-1" (Without You-1) and "Sensiz-2" (Without You-2), where I played the role of Jamshid. After that, I appeared in the TV series "Tuhmat" (Slander) as Javohir, directed by Umid Zakirov.

Movies and TV Series

Movies and TV series I've acted in

Save me

The TV series "Meni Asra" (Save Me) often covers themes such as family values, love, betrayal, the search for justice, and the complexities of human relationships. Through the life trials faced by the characters and their attempts to overcome them, the series showcases various social strata and their problems to the audience.

Without You-1

The film usually covers themes such as human relationships, love, separation, life's difficulties, and overcoming them. The plot is often built around the complex destinies of the main characters, the trials they face on their life paths, and emotional conflicts. As the film's title suggests, it's about the difficulties of living "Sensiz"

Without You-2

The TV series "Sensiz 2" typically continues the events of the first season and portrays family relationships, love, betrayal, secrets, revenge, forgiveness, misunderstandings, and their impact on people's lives. The plot reflects the complex destinies of the characters, their inner struggles, and their aspirations to overcome life's obstacles.

Slander

In Uzbek films and TV series on the theme of "Tuhmat" renowned actors and actresses often play roles. Through their performances, the characters' experiences and psychological states are deeply revealed. These films encourage viewers to be wary of slander in life, always seek the truth, and avoid harming people's reputation.

Live for me

The TV series "Men uchun yasha" (Live for Me), like other Uzbek dramatic series, covers the following themes: Family values and conflicts: The series depicts family relationships, intergenerational problems, mother-in-law and daughter-in-law conflicts, and disagreements between siblings. Love and betrayal: The main characters' love stories, the bitter consequences of betrayal.

Love me

The series typically aims to draw the audience to the screen with complex plotlines, emotional moments, and unexpected twists. The title "Meni Sev" (Love Me) also refers to one of the most important needs in human relationships: giving and receiving love.
